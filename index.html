About Me

After being graduated from a bachelor at the Sorbonne University in Economics, I decided to pursue in the Finance field. It is where I discovered computer science via a Python/R course. I instantly loved it and saw the possibilities of using machines to execute tasks. Thus, my desire to work in Finance diminished and as I was looking to learn more Python, I found the Data industry. I fell in love with the amount of ressources, concepts and was amazed when I understood that data was everywhere, in everything. Therefore, as I was finishing my Master's Degree, I knew that I didn't want to work in finance, so I enrolled in another Master's Degree, but this time, in Data Science. From this moment, I just ketp learning the field, everything continues to amaze me. From basic coding with the Pandas library to deploy machine learning models as a docker image, passing by linear algebra or computational graphs to understand how a neural network works, I just love learning and applying anyhthing from this domain.
